Have Computing Power to Spare?

The Human Genome Project took millions of dollars and years to complete.

This was largely due to the sheer amount of computational power required. Instead of running entirely on their own clusters, wouldn't it have been cool if you and I could have contributed power from our own computers? Download CrowdCrunch now and contribute to the important scientific projects of our age.
